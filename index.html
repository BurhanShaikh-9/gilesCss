AWARDS & RECOGNITION

WHY WE DO WHAT WE DO

The U.S. healthcare system is broken and businesses are paying the price.

Every year, companies spend more on employee benefits,

yet employees still struggle to afford care. HR Leaders are left managing confusing plans with rising costs and little support,

all while traditional brokers continue pushing one-size-fits-all solutions that benefit insurers more than employers.

That’s why CBG was created.

We were tired of the waste, the complexity, and the lack of transparency. We knew there had to be a better way, one that puts employers and employees first.
